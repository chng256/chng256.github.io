Research

Interests: algebraic topology, equivariant cohomology, things that make me go "ooh!"

My research is in the area of algebraic topology. My interests include equivariant cohomology and other equivariant phenomena. Currently I am computing the Mackey-functor valued Bredon homology of a point, specifically for the non-abelian group of order 21.
